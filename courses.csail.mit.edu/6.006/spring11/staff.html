6.006: Introduction to Algorithms

Contacting the staff
Do you need help with something? Which resource you should use depends on the nature of your problem. For questions about the subject matter, try looking to see if a similar question has already been asked on Piazzza first; and if you can't find an answer, ask a new question there. This will allow us to prepare (and refine) one authoritative answer to each question, and may keep you from having to wait for an email reply!
If your problem is of a personal or individual nature, you should most likely begin by contacting your TA individually. For administrative concerns (quiz conflicts, switching recitations, and so forth), contact Kevin, who will be handling these sorts of issues. The 6.006-staff mailing list will reach all members of the course staff, and the 6.006-profs mailing list will reach only the lecturers.
